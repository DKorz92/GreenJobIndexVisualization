How to become a greener economy?

Sustainability is one of the most used and most important topics of the modern times.

  • How to use the resources of our planet the most efficient way?
  • How to leave the next generation an earth to life?

Over half the of the worlds population lives in cities, so we should take these as beginning point for a more sustainable future.

Sustainability could be seen in many different ways, we take a look on green economies. The Green Jobs Index (introduced by S.T. Shutters et al. 2015) is an indicator for the current situation of a city and gives decission makers information how to develope their city in a greener, more sustainable direction.

This website is designed to help you investigate, understand and improve the standpoint of your city and their economy.
